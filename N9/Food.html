FOOD AND BEVERAGES

Nasi Kandar NS

One of the best nasi kandar in town. The price is affordable. But you have to be patience as you need to queue and wait for your turn. Must try if you visit here. Worth the long queue.

Kafe Kampung Kaw

All the food there was delicious especially the popular nasi lemak chicken chop, kambing bakar and roti gemuk with curry kambing. This is the principal menu that is always ordered by visitors

Haji Shariff’s Cendol

A small bowl of cendol with average price (RM3/bowl). It is a must to try out when you are in Negeri Sembilan. It is definitely a great bowl of cendol.

Sabak Salai Restaurant

Must try the daging salai here. Simply Delicious. Finish off with a glass of coconut shake, the combination is so amazing. Both are so good and addicting.

Leha World Nasi Campur Restaurant

Leha World Nasi Campur Restaurant is one of the best places to eat in Negeri Sembilan. There are variaties of delicious and legend food in Negeri Sembilan that you can enjoy with your family and friends.

Mr Manshor Kopitiam Power

Had to try it as its only served here. If you are a coffee lover, this is a must try. The pure coffee and milk do make a nice combo. Thirst quencher on a hot sunny day. Try it once but be prepared for a long queue as they do the drinks in batches. They also sell dessert and food here.

Anjung Selera Restaurant

This is not the high-end fine dining experience, but fantastic tasting seafood at a very reasonable price. If you are looking for a variety of delicious seafood and don't care about the very basic setting of the restaurant and not so nice restrooms, then this is the place to go.

King’s Char Kuey Teow

A very Good Malay style Char Kuey Teow. The taste is fantastic. Try the special with an extra egg on top. Open air hawker style. Really tasty. Totally different from the Chinese style. Highly recommend for anyone staying or passing by here.
